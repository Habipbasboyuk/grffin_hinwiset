MY CAREER

Griffin Hinwiset is a passionate karting driver with a remarkable journey through the competitive karting world. His racing career began in 2018 in the X30 Mini category, where he laid the foundation for his motorsport ambitions. In 2019, he quickly progressed to the X30 Junior class, demonstrating his skill by securing a 3rd place in the Dutch-Belgian Championship.

The years 2020 and 2021 presented significant challenges, with limited racing opportunities forcing Griffin to temporarily step away from the sport due to financial constraints.

However, his passion for karting never wavered. At the end of the 2024 season, Griffin made a triumphant return to competitive racing, stepping up to the X30 Senior category in the IAME Series Benelux.

His comeback has been nothing short of remarkable, Griffin has shown that he is still a fast and competitive driver, ready to make his mark in the senior karting scene in the IAME X30 Senior,

Griffin is poised to continue his racing journey with determination in 2025
